About Car Care Clinic

Capital Honda is part of the KUN Auto Group. Since inception in 2007, KUN Auto Group has forged reputation as one of the leading dealers across Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh.Capital Honda commenced operations in 2007 at Meenambakkam, Chennai. Since then, we have expanded to OMR - Chennai, Trichy, Puducherry, Thanjavur and Cuddalore. Each of our facilities is a state of art facility with well trained sales & service team.The facilities offer world class service – 3S - Sales / Service / Spares – designed to offer customers a complete solution. Our trained professional team – Sales and Service staff – and equipments deliver unparalleled Sales and Service quality and make your experience at Capital Honda a delightful one.
